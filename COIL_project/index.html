Crystal Crafts

Rules

  • Divide into two teams
  • One from each team chooses a random crystal and put it on the ground as the base layer
  • Two team or components
  • Taking turns on pulling a card and placing a crystal
  • The placement of the crystal should match the card
    e.g. if the crystal is vertical on the card it should be placed vertically on the stack
  • Be aware of any limitations instructed on the card! E.g. using your non-dominant hand (ND) or using your elbows
  • You loose if you tilt the pile or if the opponent is placing the last available crystal sucessfully
